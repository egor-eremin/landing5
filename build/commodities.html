Commodity

On the commodity market, assets are traded that we encounter to some extent in everyday life. We are talking about oil, gas, wheat, sugar, juice, etc. even without in-depth knowledge of fundamental and technical analysis, you will be able to predict the price movement on the commodity asset market.

Start trading

Predictability

There are regular and cyclical price movements on the commodity market: it is enough just to study the news to understand when to make a deal.

Prospects

Leverage instrument will allow you to use an amount several times higher than the size of your deposit for trading.

Opportunities

Commodities are a wide group of assets, each with its own characteristics and specifics, and each, with the right strategy, will bring you income.

Availability

The commodity market, like Forex, is the best place for novice traders, due to the minimum requirements for experience and the deposit amount.

Commodity market

The commodity market is ideally suited for conservative traders who want to receive a stable income. Here you will not be able to invest money in a startup, having received hundreds of percent of profit, but the cyclicity and simplicity will allow you to earn on a regular basis.
